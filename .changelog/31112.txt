```release-note:breaking-change
resource/aws_rds_cluster: The `engine` argument is now required and has no default
```

```release-note:note
data-source/aws_rds_cluster: Configurations not including the `engine` argument must be updated to include the specific engine you want. Previously, not including `engine` was equivalent to `engine = "aurora"` and created a MySQL-5.6-compatible cluster
```
